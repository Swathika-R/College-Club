Fine Arts & Dance Club

Fine Arts Dance

Grooves is the dance club of Sri Eshwar. Grooves flash mobs are legendary at Sri Eshwar. Our dancers perform during many college events and also represent our institution in inter-college culturals.

Harmonix is our music club! All students interested in singing or playing instruments are welcome to join. Harmonix entertains students with live band performances during fests and special events.
